DownloadIt
==========

DownloadIt is an unofficial modern Firefox port based on the original
FlashGot extension, which is Copyright (C) Giorgio Maone and distributed
under GPL-2.0-or-later.

FlashGot.exe
===============

FlashGot.exe is based on Grabby-FlashGot by pouriap and benzBerake:
https://github.com/benzBerake/Grabby-FlashGot

Bundled source version: v0.60.1 plus the file-based JSON CLI adaptation.
Upstream license: GPL-3.0.
The SHA-256 of the bundled binary is recorded in the generated
chrome/content/DownloadItBinaryMetadata.sys.mjs file inside each XPI.
